About

I have been employed full-time as a web designer for a little over 22 years now, most of which have been spent at Sallie Mae. My projects have included graphic design (logos, branding), UX / UI design and layout (wireframes, visual comps), front-end development (HMTL, CSS, Javascript), content management, and brand quality assurance. By working closely with others in a team-oriented environment, I have helped to build, promote, and maintain Sallie Mae's online presense as well as many of its subsidiary and partner sites.

Outside of my full-time position at Sallie Mae, I have also taken on numerous freelance jobs doing web and logo design.

I was born in Cincinnati, OH and raised in southeastern Indiana near the tri-state area. I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Graphics from Purdue University. After college, I moved to Indianapolis and now currently live in Noblesville, IN, just north of Indy.
